Adebe Bikila

The barefoot runner

Bikila during the marathon in Rome-1960

Time line of Bikila's life:

  • 1932

    Adebe Bikila was born in Ethiopia the 7th August, the same day of the Los Angeles Olimpic Marathon

  • 1960

    Bikila got the gold medal in the Olimpic Games in Rome. He ran 42,195 m. without shoes

  • 1964

    Bikila won his second gold medal in Tokio. He won twice in Olimpic Games. He stablished a New World Record in the marathon running in 2h12'12"

  • 1973

    Bikila died in Ethiopia. He died at the age of 41 due to paralysis caused by a traffic accident in his country.
